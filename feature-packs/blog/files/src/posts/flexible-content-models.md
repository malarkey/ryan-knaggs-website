A good starter does not force a content model that only works for one client. It gives you a few clear patterns and leaves enough room to adapt them.

In this project, the homepage, standard pages, posts, and team entries are deliberately separate. That makes it easier to reshape the CMS collection by collection instead of rewriting everything at once.

If a content type is not needed, delete it early and remove the navigation link at the same time.
